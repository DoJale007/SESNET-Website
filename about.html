About SESNET-Africa

Founded in May 2017 in Ghana, SESNET-Africa is a Not-for-Profit Members Organization working to market sustainable sanitation facilities (toilets) across West Africa and the continent.

Our foundation members are CleaNext Limited and WasteCare Associates — both limited liability companies in Ghana with extensive experience in the sanitation sector.

Our Objectives

  • Engage sustainable enterprise-solution providers across Africa
  • Provide a platform for sharing innovative sanitation solutions
  • Accelerate home latrine upgrades to meet SDGs
  • Serve as an online market hub for toilet providers
  • Foster communication and best practices among stakeholders
  • Establish global linkages with similar organizations
  • Offer consultancy and support to members
  • Create jobs for youth in sanitation construction and marketing
