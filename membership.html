Membership

Being a member of SESNET-Africa comes with a lot of benefits including:

  • Free online advert/marketing of sanitation (toilet facility) products
  • Free access to our help or support services
  • Networking and sharing ideas with other members through SESNET-Africa meetings and online platforms
  • Assurance of ‘value-for-money’ for clients/buyers when purchasing members’ sanitation products, which meet both national and international standards

To become a member of SESNET-Africa, kindly download and complete the membership application form and send via email to info@sesnet-africa.com.

Alternatively, completed forms may be sent by post to:

SESNET-Africa, P.O. Box LG 486, Legon, Accra-Ghana.

Applicants should include pictures of their toilet facility products.

Your application will be reviewed and feedback on membership acceptance sent via your email or contact information within five (5) working days.
